Bio: Dr. Yinghui Wu is a Theodore L. and Dana J.Schroeder Associate Professor in the Department of Computer and Data Sciences, Case School of Engineering in the Case Western Reserve University. He holds a joint position as a staff scientist at the Pacific Northwest National Laboratory.  His area is in Data management, Knowledge engineering, Databases, Data Mining and Machine Learning, with a focus on graph data analytics. Dr. Yinghui Wu directs the Database group@CWRU, and co-directs the Materials Data Science Center of Excellence (MDS3)@CWRU.

Research Interests: Data & Knowledge Management, Data Mining, Machine Learning & AI, Information Quality

To Prospective Students: I am looking for highly motivated and hardworking students who are interested in tackling data challenges (theory, algorithms, systems) including knowledge and data management, ML/AI, and security. If you are interested, please send me your CV, sample publications (if any), and transcripts.
